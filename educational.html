Educational Informational as a Student of Tangub City Global College



As a student of Tangub City Global College, education is more than completing academic requirements—it is about embracing growth, responsibility, and lifelong learning. TCGC provides an environment where students are encouraged to develop their skills, strengthen their character, and prepare for future careers. Being part of this institution means becoming globally competent, morally grounded, and technologically equipped. At TCGC, students are taught to value integrity, ensuring that every task is done honestly; compassion, showing care for others; and excellence, striving to give the best in academics and personal development. Through classroom learning, hands-on activities, and community involvement, students gain the confidence and knowledge they need to succeed in the modern world. Learning also extends beyond the campus. Whether mastering programming languages like C, understanding website design through CSS, engaging in advocacy, or participating in school activities, a TCGC student continuously grows in different aspects of life. With guidance from educators and support from the school community, every student has the opportunity to become a responsible leader and a bright light in society—truly embodying the motto of being “Lux Mundi” — the Light of the World.
